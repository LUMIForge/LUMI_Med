Privacy And Local Data

LUMI Med is an offline, local-first app. Medication, record, care plan, review, reminder, profile, image, document, and preference data is stored locally on your device by the app. The current app does not require an account and does not provide cloud sync, analytics, advertising, tracking, or developer-operated servers for this app data.

Data leaves your device only when you intentionally export, back up, restore, share, email, or send it to a destination you choose through iOS or another service.

Read the LUMI Med Privacy Policy for details about local storage, permissions, export, deletion, and regional privacy choices.

Troubleshooting

  • Notifications: confirm reminders are enabled in LUMI Med and that notifications are allowed in iOS Settings.
  • Camera or Photo Library: confirm permission is allowed in iOS Settings if you want to add an avatar or medication image.
  • Face ID or Touch ID: confirm biometric authentication is enabled on your device and that local app lock is enabled in LUMI Med.
  • Exports or backups: check the destination app, Files folder, or share sheet location you selected.
  • Display or language: update to the latest app version and latest supported iOS version when possible.

Data Deletion And Exported Files

You can clear local LUMI Med data from Settings inside the app. This deletes local app data stored by LUMI Med on the device.

If you exported a PDF, backup, image, document, or other file outside the app, delete that file separately from the location where you saved or shared it.

If you need privacy help, contact LUMIForge@hotmail.com.

Medical And Emergency Support

LUMI Med support is not an emergency service and does not provide medical advice.

Dose statistics and medication usage summaries are based only on records and dose text that you enter or mark as completed. They are not dosage calculators, dose recommendations, dose adjustments, clinical decisions, or medication safety checks.

For urgent medical help, contact your local emergency services immediately. For medication, dosage, treatment, safety, side effect, or clinical questions, consult a licensed doctor, pharmacist, or qualified healthcare professional.

Accessibility And Product Feedback

We welcome feedback about readability, accessibility, localization, and reliability. Please avoid sending sensitive health details unless they are necessary for the support request.

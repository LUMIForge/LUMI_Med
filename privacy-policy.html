LUMI Med Privacy Policy

Private medication and care records, stored locally.

LUMI Med helps you organize medications, records, reviews, care plans, reminders, documents, and reports on your device. The app is designed as an offline, local-first app with no account requirement, no cloud sync, no advertising, no analytics, and no tracking.

1. Contact, Scope, And Data Controller

This Privacy Policy explains how LUMI Med handles information in the LUMI Med iOS app and on these support pages.

For privacy, support, or data deletion questions, contact LUMI Med Support.

LUMI Med is a personal recording, reminder, and organization tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis, treatment, prescription, dose recommendation, dose calculation, dose adjustment, medication safety verification, or medical advice.

Important: app data you create in LUMI Med is stored locally on your device by the app. The developer of LUMI Med does not operate accounts, cloud sync, analytics, advertising, tracking, or developer-operated servers for this app data in the current version.

2. Information Stored On Your Device

LUMI Med stores the information you choose to enter or create in the app. This may include:

  • Medication names, dosage text, categories, stock, expiration dates, notes, and medication images.
  • Medication records, planned, due, completed, or missed status, record dates, dose details, care context, and record notes.
  • Care plans, linked medications, contraindication notes, care documents, care notes, and review activity.
  • Review records, review dates, review type, review status, outcome, provider or facility text, related documents, and review notes.
  • Reminder schedules, notification preferences, profile information, avatar, language settings, and optional local lock settings.
  • Local backup files and PDF reports if you choose to create or export them.

Some of this information may be health-related or sensitive. You control what you enter, attach, export, share, or delete.

LUMI Med does not require account registration and does not ask for a birth date, government ID, payment card, precise location, contacts, advertising identifier, or social profile.

3. Purpose And Legal Basis

Your data is used to provide app features you request, including medication lists, local reminders, records, care plans, reviews, statistics, local backups, PDF exports, profile preferences, and app settings.

For users in regions with privacy laws such as the GDPR or UK GDPR, the primary legal basis is that local processing is necessary to provide the app features you choose to use. Where a feature depends on an iOS permission, such as Notifications, Camera, Photo Library, or Face ID, that feature is used only after you allow or enable it.

LUMI Med does not use your data for advertising, cross-app tracking, third-party analytics, automated decision-making, profiling, or selling personal information.

4. Local Storage, Sharing, And International Transfers

Medication, record, care plan, review, reminder, profile, avatar, image, document, and preference data is stored locally on your device by the app. The current app is offline and local-first: it does not provide cloud sync, an account system, third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or developer-operated servers for this app data.

LUMI Med does not sell personal data and does not share medication or health data with third parties from within the app. Data leaves your device only when you intentionally use system sharing, backup, export, restore, email, messaging, Files, iCloud, AirDrop, or another destination you choose.

If you use iCloud, device backup, email, cloud storage, or another third-party service outside LUMI Med, that service may process exported or backed-up data under its own terms and privacy policy. LUMI Med does not control those external services.

Because LUMI Med does not operate cloud sync for app data in the current version, the developer of LUMI Med does not intentionally transfer your in-app medication or health records across borders. Email you send to support may be processed by the email provider used to receive and respond to your request.

5. Permissions

  • Notifications: used to send local medication or review reminders you create or enable.
  • Photo Library: used when you choose an avatar or medication image.
  • Camera: used when you choose to take an avatar or medication photo.
  • Face ID or Touch ID: used only if you enable local app lock features. Biometric authentication is handled by iOS; LUMI Med does not receive or store your biometric data.

You can manage permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

6. Retention, Deletion, And Your Privacy Choices

Your data remains on your device until you edit it, delete it, clear all local data, uninstall the app, or restore from a backup.

You can clear all local LUMI Med data from Settings. This permanently deletes local medication data, records, care plans, reviews, reminders, profile details, avatar data, images, documents, and local lock settings stored by LUMI Med on the device.

If you exported files or backups outside the app, you must delete those files separately from the place where you saved or shared them.

Depending on your region, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, objection, or information about personal data processed by a business. Because LUMI Med stores app records locally and does not operate an account database for this data, most access, correction, export, and deletion controls are available directly on your device. For privacy questions, contact LUMI Med Support.

For California users, LUMI Med does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising and does not use sensitive personal information to infer characteristics.

7. Security

LUMI Med uses iOS platform protections for local app storage and optional local app lock features when enabled. You should also protect your device with a strong passcode, keep iOS updated, and be careful when exporting or sharing files that may contain health or medication information.

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure. If you believe your data or device has been compromised, review your device security settings and contact support if the issue relates to LUMI Med.

8. Children

LUMI Med is not directed to children and is not intended for unsupervised use by children. Medication and health information should be managed carefully with appropriate parent, guardian, caregiver, and qualified healthcare guidance.

LUMI Med does not knowingly collect children's personal information through an account, cloud, advertising, or analytics system.

9. Medical Disclaimer

LUMI Med is for personal recording, reminder, statistics, and organization purposes only. It does not diagnose conditions, prescribe medication, recommend doses, calculate medication dosage, adjust dosage, verify medication safety, or replace professional medical advice.

Dose statistics, dose summaries, medication usage summaries, and medication timelines are generated only from records and dose text that you enter or mark as completed. They are historical record summaries, not dosage calculators, dose recommendations, clinical decisions, or medication safety checks.

Always consult a licensed doctor, pharmacist, or qualified healthcare professional before making medical decisions, changing medication, changing dosage, or stopping treatment. In an emergency, contact local emergency services immediately.

10. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features, legal requirements, or data practices change. The effective date will be updated when changes are made. If a future version adds accounts, cloud sync, analytics, advertising, or third-party data sharing, this policy and App Store privacy disclosures must be updated before release.

For support, privacy questions, or data deletion help, visit the LUMI Med Support page or email support.

LUMI Med 是一款离线、本地优先的个人用药与照护记录应用，用于帮助用户在自己的设备上管理药品信息、本地提醒、用药记录、照护计划、复查、文件和报告。LUMI Med 不是医疗器械，不提供诊断、治疗、处方、剂量推荐、剂量计算、剂量调整、药物安全验证或医疗建议。

1. 联系方式、范围与数据控制者

本隐私政策说明 LUMI Med iOS 应用及支持页面如何处理信息。如有隐私、支持或数据删除问题，请联系 LUMI Med 支持。

重要说明：您在 LUMI Med 中创建的应用数据由本应用存储在您的设备本地。LUMI Med 当前不为这些应用数据提供账户、云同步、第三方分析、广告、追踪或开发者运营的服务器服务。

2. 存储在您设备上的信息

LUMI Med 会存储您主动在应用中输入或创建的信息，可能包括药品名称、剂量文本、分类、库存、有效期、备注、药品图片、用药记录、照护计划、复查、提醒计划、个人资料、头像、语言设置、可选本地锁定设置、本地备份和 PDF 报告。

其中部分信息可能属于健康相关或敏感信息。您可以控制输入、附加、导出、分享或删除的内容。

LUMI Med 不要求注册账户，也不会要求您提供出生日期、政府证件、支付卡、精确位置、通讯录、广告标识符或社交账号。

3. 数据用途与法律依据

您的数据仅用于提供您选择使用的应用功能，包括药品列表、本地提醒、记录、照护计划、复查、统计、本地备份、PDF 导出、个人资料偏好和应用设置。

对于适用 GDPR 或类似隐私法律的地区，本地处理的主要依据是为了提供您选择使用的应用功能。对于通知、相机、相册、Face ID 或 Touch ID 等系统权限，只有在您允许或启用后才会使用。

LUMI Med 不会将您的数据用于广告、跨应用追踪、第三方分析、自动化决策、画像分析或出售个人信息。

4. 本地存储、共享与跨境传输

LUMI Med 会将药品、记录、照护计划、复查、提醒、个人资料、头像、图片、文件和偏好数据存储在您的设备本地。当前应用是离线、本地优先的应用，不为这些应用数据提供云同步、账户系统、第三方分析、广告、追踪或开发者运营的服务器服务。

LUMI Med 不出售您的个人数据，也不会在应用内向第三方共享您的药品或健康数据。只有在您主动使用系统分享、备份、导出、恢复、邮件、信息、文件、iCloud、AirDrop 或其他您选择的目的地时，数据才会离开您的设备。

如果您在 LUMI Med 外部使用 iCloud、设备备份、邮件、云存储或其他第三方服务，相关服务可能会按照其自身条款和隐私政策处理您导出或备份的数据。LUMI Med 无法控制这些外部服务。

5. 权限

  • 通知：用于发送您创建或启用的本地用药或复查提醒。
  • 相册：用于您从相册选择头像或药品图片。
  • 相机：用于您拍摄头像或药品照片。
  • Face ID 或 Touch ID：仅在您启用本地应用锁定功能时使用。生物识别认证由 iOS 处理，LUMI Med 不接收或存储您的生物识别数据。

您可以随时在 iOS 设置中管理权限。

6. 保留、删除与隐私选择

您的数据会保留在设备本地，直到您编辑、删除、清除所有本地数据、卸载应用或从备份恢复。

您可以在设置中清除所有 LUMI Med 本地数据。该操作会永久删除 LUMI Med 存储在本设备上的药品数据、记录、照护计划、复查、提醒、个人资料、头像数据、图片、文件和本地锁定设置。

如果您已将文件或备份导出到应用外部，需要在保存或分享的位置单独删除这些文件。

根据您所在地区，您可能享有访问、更正、删除、携带、限制、反对或了解个人数据处理情况的权利。由于 LUMI Med 将应用记录存储在本地，且不为这些数据运营账户数据库，大多数访问、更正、导出和删除操作可以直接在您的设备上完成。如有隐私问题，请联系 LUMI Med 支持。

7. 安全与儿童

LUMI Med 使用 iOS 平台的本地应用存储保护能力，并在您启用时提供本地应用锁定功能。建议您使用强设备密码、保持 iOS 更新，并谨慎导出或分享可能包含健康或用药信息的文件。

LUMI Med 不面向儿童，也不适合儿童在无人监督的情况下使用。用药和健康信息应在父母、监护人、照护者和合格医疗专业人士的适当指导下管理。

8. 医疗免责声明

LUMI Med 仅用于个人记录、提醒、统计和信息整理。它不诊断疾病、不开具药物、不推荐剂量、不计算药物剂量、不调整剂量、不验证用药安全，也不能替代专业医疗建议。

剂量统计、剂量汇总、用药汇总和用药时间线仅根据您输入或标记为已完成的记录和剂量文本生成。它们是历史记录汇总，不是剂量计算器、剂量推荐、临床决策或用药安全检查。

在做出医疗决定、改变用药、改变剂量或停止治疗前，请始终咨询执业医生、药师或合格医疗专业人士。如遇紧急情况，请立即联系当地急救服务。
